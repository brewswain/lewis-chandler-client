Lewis Chandler Construction Consulting

Lewis Chandler holds International Code Council certifications in all construction categories. He is certified in Building Plan Review, as well as Electric, Plumbing and Mechanical Plan examination. He is also an inspector - both commercial and residential, in all trades. Also, he's a former electrician and electrical contractor.

About

Mr. Chandler has been a State of Florida certified construction inspector since 1994. Today, Mr. Chandler holds 26 ICC certifications including Building Code Administrator.

Mr. Chandler served as CBO (Contract Building Official) for the Lake County School Board, CBO for the Hernandez County School Board, and performed the majority of inspections on the new Crystal River High School in Citrus County, Florida.

Mr Chandler was also the Contract Building Official for the cities of:

  • Inverness - 3 Years 6 months.
  • Brooksville - 5 Years.
  • Tavares - 3 Years 6 months.
  • Bushnell - 6 Years approximately prior to retirement.
This new endeavour is specifically planned to assist Engineers, Architects, Contractors, and Owners/Builders with their construction projects. Where possible, we will help mitigate construction costs or provide alternative means and methods.

Mr. Chandler also holds Zoning and property Standards, and Coastal Construction. No matter where you choose to build in Florida, and whatever type of construction you choose, Mr. Chandler has been involved, from tilt wall to post tension to simple balloon framing. He has experienced it all.
If you need MEP control or just an overall plan review, he can provide you whatever level of service you need.
